Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?

Strategic District is free to start. Steps 1-7 are included at no cost. Enterprise starts at $5,000 per year and unlocks the full 14-step process, Strategic School, and professional exports. Save with multi-year plans.

Do we need training?

Our step-by-step builder is designed to be intuitive. Most teams don't need training. We provide clear instructions and examples throughout every step.

How long does it take?

Most districts complete their strategic plan in 4-6 weeks of active work. The free foundation steps can be done in a few focused sessions.

What is Strategic School?

Strategic School is our school-level planning tool included with Enterprise. It lets district leaders create School Improvement Plans that automatically align to the district's strategic plan.

Can we export our plan?

Yes. Enterprise users can export their complete strategic plan to PDF, Word, and web formats. Professional reports ready for your board and community.

Is our data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share or sell district information. Your plan belongs to you.

Can we update our plan later?

Absolutely. Strategic District is built for continuous improvement. Come back any time to update a section, refresh the whole plan, or create new school plans.

What data gets auto-populated?

When you select your district in Step 1, we pull publicly available data including enrollment, demographics, and school counts. You can edit or add to this at any time.

How many plans can we create?

Free accounts can create one strategic plan. Enterprise accounts can create unlimited district and school plans.

Who built this?

Strategic District was built by Stark Analytica LLC, a company founded by and staffed entirely with former educators and district leaders. We built this because we lived the strategic planning process ourselves and knew it could be better.
