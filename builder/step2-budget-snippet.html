💡 Budget Planning Best Practices

  • Identify all funding sources: Title funds, IDEA, federal grants, state allocations, bond funds, and general fund allocations.
  • Account for restrictions: Some funding sources (Title I, IDEA) have specific use restrictions.
  • Plan for sustainability: Federal grant funds may expire. Build in contingency.
  • Align with priorities: Ensure your budget allocation matches your strategic priorities and goals.
  • Leave flexibility: Reserve 5-10% for mid-course adjustments and emerging needs.
