District Profile: Enrollment Trends

Greenfield Unified School District's strategic plan charts a clear course for elevating student outcomes across all 120 schools. Built on three years of data analysis, stakeholder engagement, and industry best practices, this plan positions the district for sustained success.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Equitable Access and Excellence in Learning
  • High-Performance Teaching and Leadership
  • Data-Driven Decision Making
  • Family and Community Partnerships

The plan commits the district to an ambitious but achievable 90% graduation rate within four years, backed by evidence-based initiatives and dedicated resource allocation.

Goals and Measures: Baseline vs Target

Four strategic domains anchor our work, each with specific, measurable goals:

  • Academic Excellence: 90% graduation rate by 2028; 85% proficiency in core subjects
  • Equity and Access: Close achievement gaps by 25%; expand AP/advanced course enrollment
  • Teacher Excellence: 95% effective/highly effective teacher ratings; reduce vacancy rate to 3%
  • Community Engagement: 80% family participation in district initiatives

Progress tracked quarterly through data dashboards with principal accountability and resource support.

Central Office Alignment

Every department in the central office is aligned to at least one strategic goal. This ensures the entire organization is pulling in the same direction and schools get the support they need.

Curriculum and Instruction

Aligned to Academic Excellence goals. Leads standards-based curriculum review and professional development.

Human Resources

Aligned to Teacher Excellence goals. Leads teacher pipeline, retention strategies, and residency programs.

Student Services

Aligned to Equity and Access goals. Coordinates wraparound supports, counseling, and family engagement hubs.

Technology

Aligned to Data-Driven Decision Making. Deploys dashboards, data platforms, and digital learning tools.

Finance and Operations

Aligned to all domains. Ensures resources are allocated strategically to support plan priorities.

Communications

Aligned to Community Engagement goals. Manages public reporting, stakeholder updates, and feedback loops.

Each department submits quarterly progress reports showing how their work directly supports strategic plan goals.

Implementation Initiatives: 2026-2028

We'll achieve our goals through targeted, evidence-based initiatives:

  • Early Warning System: Identify at-risk students by 8th grade for targeted intervention
  • Teacher Residency Program: 200 new highly effective teachers over 3 years
  • Career Pathways: Industry-aligned programs in healthcare, technology, and skilled trades
  • Family Engagement Hubs: Year-round support centers in every school cluster
  • Data and Accountability Platform: Real-time dashboards for decision makers at all levels
